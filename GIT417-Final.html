About the Band

We are an American metalcore band from Richmond, Virginia, formed in 2015. We're influenced musically in ways that don't coincide with norms. Band member include Jaokim "Jolly" Karlsson, Noah Sebastian, Nick Folio and Nicholas Ruffilo.
